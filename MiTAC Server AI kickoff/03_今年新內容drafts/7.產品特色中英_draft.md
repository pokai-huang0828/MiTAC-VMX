> **MiTAC is pioneering the connected-vehicle AI industry with the world's first cloud-based AI secondary analysis platform — the "Server AI Cloud Intelligence Platform" — engineered to overcome the persistent industry pain point of high false-positive and miss-detection rates in ADAS and DMS systems caused by edge-device compute limitations.**
>
> **The challenge.** Conventional fleet safety AI has relied solely on local inference at the dashcam edge, where AI model size is capped by limited automotive-grade compute. As a result, industry-wide false-positive rates have reached 30–40%, eroding fleet managers' trust in safety alerts and increasing operational cost from chasing false alarms.
>
> **The breakthrough.** MiTAC's self-developed Server AI Platform introduces a **hybrid hardware-software architecture** combining event-triggered edge capture with cloud-based secondary AI analysis. This enables edge dashcams to access **data-center-grade visual AI inference**, previously impossible at automotive cost and power envelopes. The platform integrates MiTAC's proprietary Yawning Detection Model, G-Sensor dynamic-behavior thresholds, and ADAS road-risk algorithms — **reducing false-positive rates by over 50%** in real-world production deployments.
>
> **Five next-generation capabilities** are delivered through the platform:
>
> 1. Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS) with road-context awareness
> 2. Driver eye-stability scoring algorithm for fatigue precision
> 3. Camera auto-calibration for installation-agnostic accuracy
> 4. Driver-behavior confidence scoring model
> 5. Driver-behavior comparative analysis model
>
> **Modular architecture, open API.** Server AI's modular design and flexible API enable seamless integration with existing fleet management systems, allowing customers to extend AI capability without hardware replacement.
>
> **Production-grade global deployment.** The platform is operating in production across **four major regions — Asia, North America, Europe, and Australia** — serving large-scale logistics, public transit, construction fleet, and security fleet operators. Cross-region production data validates platform stability under diverse regulatory and operational contexts.
>
> **Certified and protected.** The platform meets ISO 9001, ISO 14001, QC 080000, CE, FCC, and WEEE standards, backed by **five Taiwan-issued AI patents** in image recognition and fleet management algorithms.
>
> **Why this matters.** Server AI Platform showcases **Taiwan's autonomous AI core competence** on a global scale, embodies the next paradigm of "**edge compute downshift, cloud intelligence upshift**" for connected vehicles, and contributes to a safer, more energy-efficient, and more sustainable future for global fleet operations.
